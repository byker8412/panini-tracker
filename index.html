What is this?

A tracker for the official Panini FIFA World Cup 2026 sticker album. All 48 teams (stickers 1–20 each) plus the 20 introduction FWC foil stickers and 12 Coca-Cola bonus stickers — 992 stickers total. Tick off what you own.

How do I tick a sticker?

Click or tap any sticker box to toggle it on ✓ owned or off □ needed. Changes save instantly.

What are the shimmering silver stickers?

Those are foil stickers. Every team badge (sticker 1) and all 20 FWC introduction stickers are foil. The shimmer continues when ticked — it just switches to silver.

What are the red stickers?

The Coca-Cola bonus stickers — found under Coca-Cola bottle labels. Twelve placeholder slots are included; tick them as you find them.

Will my ticks be saved if I close the browser?

Yes — stored in browser local storage on this device and browser. Survives closing and reopening as long as you don't clear browser data.

How do I move my progress to another device?

Click ↓ Export Progress. Downloads a new copy of the file with all your ticks baked in. Send it anywhere and your collection travels with it.

How do I filter the teams?

Incomplete Only shows teams you've started but not finished. Not Started shows zero-sticker teams. The introduction and Coca-Cola sections always stay pinned at the top.

How do I reset everything?

Click the red Reset All button and confirm. Clears ticks in this browser only — exported copies are unaffected.

Does this need internet / an account?

No. Fully offline. Open in any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. No install, no sign-in.
